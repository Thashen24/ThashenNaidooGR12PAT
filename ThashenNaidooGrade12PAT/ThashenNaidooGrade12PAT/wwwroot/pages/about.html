About Us

Hi, I am Thashen Naidoo, a Grade 12 student at Crawford College International La Lucia. I am organizing a trip for me and my three friends to watch the Saudi Arabia Grand Prix. This is part of my CAT PAT project.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an unforgettable experience for Formula 1 enthusiasts. By organizing this trip, we aim to offer a unique opportunity to witness the excitement of the Grand Prix in one of the most vibrant locations on the racing calendar.

Our Journey

The idea for this trip came from our shared love for Formula 1 and the desire to experience a Grand Prix in person. After months of planning and preparation, we are excited to see our vision come to life in Saudi Arabia. Our journey has been filled with enthusiasm, teamwork, and a lot of racing trivia!
